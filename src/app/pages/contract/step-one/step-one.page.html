BASIC VERIFICATION Create Your Bid

First thing is some basic legal stuff to ensure that you are not acting as an agent for someone else, that you are capable of understanding and signing this offer, and that you have had an opportunity to view the house. (Whether you actually took that opportunity is irrelevant at this time)

As the signatory party to this bid, I verify that I have had an opportunity to, or forego the chance to, inspect and scrutinize the property subject to this bid along with all associated legal documents including but not limited to, plan of survey, inspection reports, appraisals, deeds, and status cerificates.

I verifythat all parties signatory to this offer have the mental capacity to read and sign this offer and that those same parties possess the financial means to pursue the intent of purchasing this property.

I verify that I am not submitting this bid on behalf of another as a part of an agency or customer-relationship wherein I am acting as an intermediary. I further acknowledge that in signing this document all signatory parties must have legal stake in the property.
